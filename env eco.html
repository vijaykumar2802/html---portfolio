Introduction to Environmental Economics - NPTEL (IIT)

January - April 2024 | 12-week course

I completed the NPTEL course on Introduction to Environmental Economics, which explored the intersection of economic theory and environmental policy. The course provided a strong foundation in understanding how economic tools can be used to address environmental challenges, evaluate the costs and benefits of environmental actions, and develop sustainable solutions.

Key concepts and learnings:

  • Fundamentals of environmental economics and the role of externalities.
  • Valuation of environmental goods and ecosystem services.
  • Cost-benefit analysis and market-based instruments for pollution control.
  • Policy frameworks for sustainable development and resource management.
  • Case studies demonstrating the application of economic principles in real-world environmental issues.

The course strengthened my analytical and policy-oriented thinking by combining economic reasoning with environmental stewardship.
